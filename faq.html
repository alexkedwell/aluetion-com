We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we hear from business owners and teams considering automation.

💰 Pricing & Plans

How much does it cost? ▾
Our standard automation plan is $197/month for one location. This covers up to 10 automation workflows including appointment reminders, follow-ups, review requests, and more. Setup and onboarding are included. No hidden fees.
Is there a free trial? ▾
We don't offer a free trial, but we do offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not seeing results within your first two weeks, just let us know and we'll refund you, no questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Just email us at hello@aluetion.com and we'll cancel your subscription immediately. We'll also export all your data so you have it.
What if I have multiple locations? ▾
The $197/mo plan covers one location. For multiple locations, we'll create a custom quote that scales with your business. Email hello@aluetion.com and we'll put something together for you.
Are there setup fees? ▾
No setup fees. We handle all the configuration and onboarding as part of your subscription. We'll get you live within 24–48 hours at no extra cost.

⚙️ Setup & Integration

How long does setup take? ▾
Most clients are live within 24–48 hours. We handle the entire setup. All we need is access to your booking system and a few details about your messaging preferences. Once we have that, we configure everything and test it before you go live.
What booking systems do you work with? ▾
We work with most major booking platforms including Mindbody, Acuity, Jane App, Google Calendar, Calendly, and more. If you use something else, just ask. We can usually make it work. If we don't support your system yet, we can often build a custom integration.
Do you need access to my account? ▾
We'll ask for API access or limited admin access to set up the integrations. This is the secure way to connect your systems. We never store passwords or sensitive data. You can revoke our access anytime in your booking system settings.
Can you integrate with my CRM? ▾
Yes. We integrate with major CRM platforms and can build custom connections to most others. Email hello@aluetion.com with details about your CRM and we'll let you know what's possible.
What if something breaks? ▾
Our AI agents monitor your automations 24/7 and alert us to any issues. If something goes wrong, we fix it immediately. You'll also get a notification so you're always in the loop.

📱 Messaging & Communication

Do you send SMS and email? ▾
We currently send email and SMS through partner providers. Costs vary by volume. We'll show you exactly what to expect before setup. For SMS, most businesses pay $0.01–$0.05 per message depending on volume.
Do you write the messages for me? ▾
Yes. Our team writes and configures all the messaging in your brand voice. You review and approve everything before it goes live. We handle updates too, so you don't have to think about it.
Can I customize the messages? ▾
Absolutely. You can customize timing, tone, and content. Need to add your business name, discount code, or custom text? Easy. We can also set up conditional messages based on client data.
What about compliance (TCPA, GDPR)? ▾
We follow all applicable regulations including TCPA for SMS, GDPR for email, and CASL in Canada. We get proper consent, include unsubscribe options, and maintain compliance documentation. We handle the legal details so you don't have to worry.
Can clients opt out? ▾
Yes. Every message includes unsubscribe options. Clients can opt out anytime, and we immediately stop sending them messages. You can also manage preferences in your dashboard.

🔒 Data & Privacy

Is my data secure? ▾
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2+) for data in transit and at rest. Our servers are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits. We never sell or share your data.
Who has access to my client data? ▾
Only you and our AI agents have access to your data. Our AI handles scheduling, monitoring, and basic troubleshooting. Our human team can see aggregate usage data but never access individual client details. You're always in control.
Can I export my data? ▾
Yes. You can export all your data anytime—client lists, automation history, messaging logs, everything. We'll give it to you in standard formats (CSV, JSON) so you can move it wherever you want.
What about backups? ▾
We automatically back up your data daily and store encrypted backups in geographically distributed locations. If something goes wrong, we can restore your data with minimal downtime.
Do you comply with HIPAA? ▾
We don't currently offer HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, so we're not suitable for healthcare data. If you need HIPAA compliance, let us know and we can discuss options or point you toward alternatives.

You choose which ones to use.

Can you help me optimize? ▾
Yes. After 30 days, our team reviews your automation performance and suggests optimizations. We might adjust timing, messaging, or which automations to prioritize based on what's actually working.

How do I contact support? ▾
Email hello@aluetion.com. You can also message through your dashboard. We typically respond within 24 hours. For priority support, just mention it's urgent.
