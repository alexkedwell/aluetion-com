The Fix

Everything that keeps your chair full. automated

📲

Appointment Reminders

SMS and email reminders go out 48h and 2h before every booking. Clients can confirm with one tap. and if they need to reschedule, they can do it without a phone call, freeing up your slot in time to fill it.

🔄

Win-Back Campaigns

Client gone quiet for 60+ days? An automated win-back message goes out. friendly, personal, and without you having to think about it. On average, 22% of lapsed clients come back from a well-timed message.

🎂

Birthday Messages

On their birthday, your client gets a warm message from your shop. It's the kind of personal touch that builds real loyalty. and it's one of the easiest reasons to rebook. Most salons never do this.

⭐

Review Requests (No Awkwardness)

Two hours after a visit, clients get a friendly text asking for a Google review. It's perfectly timed, completely hands-off, and doesn't put anyone on the spot. Your stars go up, new clients find you.

📨

Rebooking Follow-Ups

After every visit, an automated follow-up gently nudges clients to book their next appointment. No chasing, no calls. just a consistent stream of rebookings filling your calendar ahead of time.

🤝

Referral Requests

Happy clients are your best marketing. Automated referral messages go out after great visits with a shareable link. turning word-of-mouth into a system instead of hoping it happens on its own.
