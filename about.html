Our Story

We started Aluetion because we watched too many small businesses struggle with the same boring problems: appointment reminders that never get sent, follow-ups that fall through the cracks, customer data scattered across five different tools.

The market was clear: big platforms wanted millions of dollars in upfront costs and months of implementation. Smaller platforms were outdated or inflexible. Nothing was built specifically for the business owner who just wanted things to work, without the enterprise tax.

So we built Aluetion. A name that means "the solution"—because that's what it is. A team that combines human expertise with AI agents to deliver automation that actually fits your business, at a price that makes sense. No bloat. No surprises. Just fast, reliable automation.
