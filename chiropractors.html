The Fix

Automated systems that keep patients on track

📲

Appointment Reminders

Every patient gets an SMS and email reminder 48h and 2h before their adjustment. They can confirm with one tap or reschedule. giving you the chance to fill the slot instead of losing it entirely.

🔁

Follow-Up Care Sequences

After each visit, patients get a check-in message. how are they feeling, when to book next, what to expect as their treatment progresses. It keeps them engaged, reinforces their plan, and prompts rebooking naturally.

🔄

Win-Back Campaigns

Patient gone 60+ days? An automated win-back sequence reaches out. no awkward calls, no chasing. Just a well-timed message that reminds them you're there. On average, 22% of lapsed patients come back.

⭐

Review Requests (Done Right)

Two hours after a visit, patients get a friendly, professionally worded text asking for a Google review. It's tasteful, appropriate for a healthcare setting, and completely automated. Reviews build trust that brings in new patients.

📋

Pre-Visit Instructions

Before certain appointments. especially first visits or post-procedure check-ins. patients automatically receive preparation notes. What to wear, what to avoid, what to expect. Fewer questions, smoother visits.

🎂

Birthday & Milestone Messages

A birthday message or care milestone note ("You've completed 10 visits. here's what that means for your progress") builds loyalty that goes beyond the adjustment table. Small touches that make patients stick around.
